代数篇3: 矩阵

设 A 是 m*n 矩阵, B 是 n*m 矩阵, 证明: tr(AB) = tr(BA).

证 设 AB 的 ij 元是 `c_(ij)`, BA 的 ij 元是 `d_(ij)`. 于是

`text(tr)(AB) = sum_(i=1)^m c_(ii) = sum_(i=1)^m sum_(j=1)^n a_(ij) b_(ji),`

`text(tr)(BA) = sum_(j=1)^n d_(jj) = sum_(j=1)^n sum_(i=1)^m b_(ji) a_(ij).`

所以 tr(AB) = tr(BA).`square`

设 `A in bbb"P"^(m xx n)`, 证明: tr(AA') = 0 当且仅当 A = O.

证 `lArr`: 显然. `rArr`: 记 AA' 的 ij 元为 `b_(ij)`, 则

`text(tr)(A A') = sum_(i=1)^m b_(ii)` `= sum_(i=1)^m sum_(j=1)^n a_(ij) a_(ji)'` `= sum_(i=1)^m sum_(j=1)^n a_(ij)^2` `= 0`.

所以 `a_(ij) = 0`; `i = 1, 2, cdots, m`; `j = 1, 2, cdots, n`. 即 A = O.`square`
